# Part 1 Manual Smoke Checklist

## Audio Shortcuts
- Mo trang `Attempt` voi de co audio.
- Bam `Space` de play/pause ma khong can focus vao thanh audio.
- Bam `ArrowLeft` / `ArrowRight` de tua lui/tien 5 giay.
- Bam `Alt + ArrowLeft` / `Alt + ArrowRight` de chuyen cau.
- Khi de khong co audio, `ArrowLeft` / `ArrowRight` van chuyen cau nhu cu.
- Dat con tro vao `textarea` note o trang `Review`, bam phim audio va xac nhan shortcut khong kich hoat.

## Review To Flashcard
- Mo `Review`, boi den text trong `prompt`, `choice`, va `explanation`.
- Xac nhan nut `+ Them flashcard` hien gan vung boi den.
- Bam nut va xac nhan modal mo ra voi `term` da duoc dien san.
- Tao flashcard tu nut `+ Flashcard` trong khu vuc explanation.
- Tao flashcard tu nut `+ Flashcard` trong khu vuc note, ca truong hop co selection trong textarea va khong co selection.

## Flashcard Workspace
- Mo route `#/flashcards`.
- Tao flashcard thu cong bang nut `Flashcard moi`.
- Tim kiem theo `term`, loc theo `deck`, `tag`, `part`, va bat `chi hien den han`.
- Sua va xoa mot flashcard trong danh sach.
- Vao study mode, xac nhan flashcard co hieu ung lat 3D ro mat truoc/mat sau, khong nhap nhay.
- Bam vao the de lat, sau do thu lat bang ban phim `Space` va `Enter` khi the dang focus.
- Xac nhan nut `Again`, `Hard`, `Good`, `Easy` bi vo hieu hoa truoc khi lat va de bam sau khi lat.
- Xac nhan progress session hien dung theo dang `the X / N` va thanh tien do cap nhat khi chuyen the.
- Thay doi bo loc (`deck`, `tag`, `part`, `search`) va xac nhan queue hoc den han thay doi theo bo loc hien tai.
- Bat che do `Reduce motion` trong he dieu hanh/trinh duyet va xac nhan lat the chuyen thanh chuyen canh nhe hon, khong gay choang.
- Xac nhan so luong due cards giam sau khi review.
